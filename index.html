Where does your
moral compass point?

Not “what would you do?” but “whose move is yours?” Each question hands you a small cast of very specific people facing the same situation. You decide whose reasoning you recognise. At the end: your compass across five axes, and the moral archetype you earned.

For each person you can lean their way (one click) or commit with conviction (two clicks). You can also mark yourself undecided, hold that there is no fact of the matter, add a …but to hedge, or skip. Some questions instead ask you to mark every view you could argue. Nothing you pick is graded right or wrong, and your answers are stored only in this browser.
